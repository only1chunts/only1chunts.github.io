Bridge End Garden is a restored Victorian Grade 11* listed garden located on the north side of Saffron Walden and open to the public year round. Made up of a series of smaller interconnecting gardens including the Dutch Garden and a mature hedge maze, it is full of surprises.

Discover the new Visitor Centre with its colourful information panels and interactive monitors. Tucked away in the Walled Garden, the Centre opened in October 2015. Admission to the Garden and Visitor Centre is free.



The Friends of Bridge End Gardens is a small charity that supports the restoration, management and future development of the Garden. We welcome new members and volunteers - for further information email friends@bridgeendgarden.co.uk or call in at Saffron Walden Tourist Information Centre in the Market Square.
